 Simple Cream Cake Instructions:

            This recipe yields a basic and delicious cream cake, 
            perfect for beginners or a quick treat. Remember to adjust quantities and
            flavors based on your preference.
       
                Preparation Time
                

                . Total: approximately 30 min.
                . Preparation: 20min.
                . cooking: 10min.

    Ingredient:

  • 1/2 cup butter, melted
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
